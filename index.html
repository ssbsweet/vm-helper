About Me

Currently, I am an UI Visual Designer with a broad range of digital design experience, who has lived in and around San Francisco most of my life.

I'm a constant learner. I'm always testing new tools and experimenting with new techniques.

I've created interactive, ui, animation, visual design and illustrations for hundreds of clients and brands such as Facebook, Williams-Sonoma, CPSC, Electronic Arts, CloudOn, PayPal, TripIt and many more.

Probably like a lot of other designers, I've had a lot of different titles as the web evolved, and the work has been called a lot of different things like Art Direction, Product Design, User Experience Design, Interaction Design, User Interface Design, Visual Design, Instructional Design, Illustrator, Interactive Design. Often the titles don't keep pace with the creative work, which is typically goal driven. You could have the title of Art Director but be writing code or just as easily be vector illustrating characters or storyboarding with concurrent style development.

I've worked with global agencies like Publicis Hal Riney on cross-media visual campaigns for global-reach companies like Paypal, UBS, and LG Electronics.

But, I've also worked with startups like CloudOn who were translating document design tools to gestural tablet interface.

I've gone to DC as a creative with instructional design skills, employed by a large contractor for the GSA, building web-based, interactive 3d learning modules for the Military's Defense Aquisition University, taking into account emerging standards.

Another creative challenge I've undertaken involved working with a software application design team to bring modern design benchmarks to a suite of well-established global software products, and to innovate for new emerging mobile platforms.

A few years ago, as another creative experiment, I started uploading my digital icons, backgrounds, and art available for sale on Creative Market, Shutterstock, iStockphoto, and Adobe Stock.

I've added a wide variety of visual digital assets such as icons, patterns, textures, sets of backgrounds, digital paintings, and complex vector art.

I have also been putting up experiments on Instagram. Thanks for stopping by to check out my work. Cheers!
